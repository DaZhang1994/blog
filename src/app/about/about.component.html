I am a traveler

When I was a little child, my favorite TV shows were travel shows. I was obsessed with exploring the world, meeting amazing people and flying from one place to another. Once, I found an old album of my grandpa on the shelf, all of the pictures were taken in Russia and Ukraine. He told me he had been to a lot of countries and cities, he even could speak Russian and Japanese. Grandpa said, "Travel is a kind of self-enhancement, I enjoyed myself in those old days, I cherish the wonderful memories, I miss my old friends..." When I grew up, I traveled across half of the China, after that, I started to plan to travel abroad...
